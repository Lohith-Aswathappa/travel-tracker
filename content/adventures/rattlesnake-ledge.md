A classic weekend hike with a reward that still feels bigger than the effort. We started late, carried too many snacks, and somehow timed the ledge perfectly for soft evening light.

The trail was busy near the bottom, but the final stretch felt calm. This is exactly the kind of place we want this archive to remember: not a huge vacation, just a beautiful Saturday that would otherwise blur into the year.
